🔄 Smart Synchronization Mechanism

Kazoku connects directly with Apple Health (iOS) and Google Fit (Android) to automatically collect your health data. These two platforms act as aggregation hubs, gathering information from multiple sources:

📱 Built-in phone sensors: Measure steps, distance traveled, floors climbed, and basic daily activities.

⌚ Smart wearable devices: Apple Watch, Fitbit, Samsung Galaxy Watch, Garmin, Huawei Watch, and hundreds of other devices record heart rate, sleep, calories burned, and many other important metrics with high accuracy.

🏃 Fitness apps: Strava, Nike Run Club, Huawei Health, and other apps automatically share workout and nutrition data.

Kazoku only needs to connect once, after that all data from these sources will be automatically and continuously synced to the app. You don't need to manually enter data or worry about updates - everything happens automatically and accurately in the background.
