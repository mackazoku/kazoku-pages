How to request account deletion

You may request deletion using either the in-app flow or by sending an email. We will verify your identity before processing the deletion.

1. From inside the app

  1. Open [Kazoku].
  2. Go to Account-Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the prompts to confirm.

2. By email (if you cannot access the app)

Send an email to our support team with the subject line: Account Deletion Request. Please include the email address associated with your account and a short statement requesting deletion.

Support email: contact@kazoku-ai.com

We will respond to verify your identity and confirm the deletion within the timeframes shown below.

What data will be deleted

When you request permanent deletion, we will remove the following data from our servers:

  • Account profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Health data synced from Health Connect (steps, heart rate, sleep, calories, exercise sessions)
  • Family group membership and shared family records (personal data will be removed; references will be anonymized)
  • App activity logs, settings, and user-generated files or reports

After deletion is complete, this data cannot be recovered.

What data may be retained and retention period

In some limited cases we may retain certain information for legal, security, or operational reasons. Examples include:

  • Transaction or billing records required by law
  • Security logs and fraud-prevention information
  • Records required for compliance with regulatory obligations

Retention period for such data is up to 30 days after the deletion request, unless a longer period is required by applicable law. After the retention period, we will securely delete or anonymize remaining data.

What happens to shared family data

If you are part of a family group within the app:

  • Your personal health data will be deleted from our servers.
  • Shared family records that referenced you will be anonymized so that other members' records remain intact.
  • Other family members' data will not be deleted due to your request.

Timeline & confirmation

We will verify your request and typically complete account deletion within 7 days. Once deletion is complete, we will send a confirmation email to the address on file.
