Featured Product: Kazoku

Smart family management app with AI

Kazoku is MAC's flagship product - a comprehensive family management application with full features to connect and organize family life in the most efficient way. Completely free and ad-free.

Key Features

  • Timeline & Chat: Manage and share household tasks, events, health diaries, and daily activities
  • Expenses: Track family spending and plan finances smartly
  • Notes & Albums: Store and share private family moments securely
  • Family AI: Connect generations through a friendly interface and intelligent AI support that learns and personalizes

Benefits

  • Save time: All family information in one place, easy to search and manage
  • Increase connection: Every member stays updated and participates in family life
  • Absolute security: Data is encrypted and only your family has access
  • Easy to use: Intuitive interface suitable for all ages from children to the elderly
